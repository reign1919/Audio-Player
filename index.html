How to Play Local Files on Spotify:

  1. Download the songs above by clicking on the Download button.
  2. Open the Spotify app and go to Settings > Local Files.
  3. Turn on the Show Local Files switch and choose the folders where your songs are stored. You can also click Add a Source to select a custom folder.
  4. Go to Your Library and find the Local Files folder in Playlists.
  5. Search for either Kompa-The Remix or empty-hope.
  6. Play and enjoy!
